# British Ministry of Magic Portal

## NPC Engine Design Document

---

# Vision

NPCs are not quest givers.

NPCs are Ministry employees.

They have careers.

Relationships.

Responsibilities.

Histories.

Memories.

The Ministry exists because they exist.

---

# Core Philosophy

The player is not the center of the world.

NPCs have lives before meeting the player.

And they continue living afterwards.

# Conversation Rules

NPCs never dump information.

They speak naturally.

Professional when working.

Casual after trust grows.

Each department has its own culture.

---

# Daily Routine

Every NPC has:

Office hours

Lunch

Meetings

Vacation

Current assignment

Mood

Energy

Availability

They are not always waiting for the player.

---

# Reputation

NPC opinion changes through actions.

Not dialogue choices.

Example:

Late reports

Successful investigations

Helping coworkers

Breaking protocol

Saving lives

Sharing evidence

Following rules

Ignoring rules
