MISSION

Create the most realistic magical archive ever experienced.

The user should feel like they are browsing a real classified government archive rather than a website.

-----------------------------------

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Minimal

Dark

Elegant

No flashy animations

Every interaction must feel authentic.

USER EXPERIENCE

Nothing should look like a game.

Nothing should look AI generated.

Everything should feel like it existed before the visitor opened the website.

-----------------------------------

INTERACTION RULES

Every click reveals information.

Every document has metadata.

Every photograph has context.

Every case references another case.

Nothing exists in isolation.

-----------------------------------

WORLD RULES

Magic leaves evidence.

Evidence can disappear.

Memories are unreliable.

Reports can contain mistakes.

Truth is reconstructed by the player.

-----------------------------------

PROJECT GOAL

When someone visits The Hidden Files,

their first reaction should be

"Is this actually real?"
